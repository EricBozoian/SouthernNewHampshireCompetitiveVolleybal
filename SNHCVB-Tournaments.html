About SNHCVB Tournaments:

Southern New Hampshire Competitive Volleyball (SNHCVB) is an independent organization that hosts co-ed, men’s, women’s, and Reverse Co-ed (RCO) volleyball tournaments. Our events provide a welcoming environment for players of all skill levels, fostering competitive play at an affordable cost. SNHCVB runs both indoor and grass volleyball tournaments, offering a variety of formats, including 6v6, 4v4, and 2v2 competitions. SNHCVB operates using a unique rating system and its own set of customized rules, designed specifically for our local tournaments. This approach ensures a balance between fun and competitive play, while maintaining fairness across all skill levels

Unique Rating System:

SNHCVB employs a customized rating scale that ensures competitive balance in all tournaments. Player ratings range from Expert Plus (XP) (8 points) down to Novice (N) (1 point). This system helps maintain fair team compositions while accommodating players of various skill levels, allowing for dynamic and competitive matches.

To read the entire rating scale, click here: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1-0kyOcbK5vgX5wO-W1czPaVyHjwzMgNHVmT8w5PM2Ow/edit?usp=sharing

Custom SNHCVB Rules:

All SNHCVB events are governed by the Southern New Hampshire Competitive Volleyball (SNHCVB) Rules 2024-2025. These rules cover indoor volleyball, co-ed play, RCO, and grass volleyball, and emphasize fairness, respect, and competitive integrity. The flexibility of these rules allows us to adapt to local needs and tournament formats, ensuring smooth operation at every event. Tournament-specific rules are provided in the event descriptions, with general guidelines applying unless otherwise specified.

To read the full rules, click here: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Q3Y1xOW3S7CAIdUYNOcIaqwXgPMctKFIWa3RsLacFLo/edit?usp=sharing
