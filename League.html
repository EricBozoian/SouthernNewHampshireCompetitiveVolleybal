SNHCVB League


Important Information

  • Cost: $471 per Team, $78 per Player
  • League Days: Thursdays, Start: Thursday, Jan 16th, 2025, End: March 27th, 2025 (subject to adjustment due to Eric's wedding)
  • Prizes: 1st place: $400, 2nd place: $150
  • How to Sign Up: Click the links below
  • Registration Deadline: Saturday, January 11th, 2025, by 5:00 PM
  • Format: 3 Teams: 2 Teams play while 1 team refs. Games to 21 points, 3 matches per night.

About the League:

Welcome to the Southern New Hampshire Competitive Volleyball (SNHCVB) League! The highest skill level permitted in the league is Advanced (A), rated at 5 points, which is equivalent to a Yankee B- level (see the rating section below for a more detailed overview). Our league offers an exciting format, generous prizes, and a fair, dynamic environment for all participants.

League Highlights:

Prizes:
First Place: $400 for the team (individual players receive $66.67 each).
Second Place: $150 for the team (individual players receive $25 each).
Both first and second-place teams will also receive trophies to commemorate their achievement.

Registration Options:
Team Registration: Flat fee of $471 per team.
Individual Player Registration: $78 per player. Individual players will be assigned to teams, and we assist in forming balanced rosters. Players unhappy with their assigned teams may request a refund up to 5 days before the league starts.

Game Schedule:
Each team plays 14 regular-season games followed by playoffs.
Playoff Format: Only the top three seeded teams advance, competing for the grand prizes.
Each team will also referee five games as part of their responsibilities.

League Capacity:
Maximum of five teams. Spots are limited, so secure your place early!

Rules & Fair Play:
Cheating of any kind is strictly prohibited and will be enforced by Eric Bozoian. Teams or players found violating the rules will receive one warning, after which participation may be forfeited without a refund. Decisions regarding forfeits will be determined at Eric's discretion.

Allowable Player Levels for the League:

SNHCVB employs a customized rating scale that ensures competitive balance in all tournaments. This system evaluates individual skill levels, helping to create fair and dynamic team compositions. The highest skill level permitted in the league is Advanced (A), rated at 5 points, which is equivalent to a Yankee B- level.

We put together a video overview of all the player ratings. If you are new to player ratings and want to see where you fit, click here: Player Ratings Video

To read the entire rating scale and see where you fit, click here: Rating Scale Document

Custom SNHCVB Rules:

All SNHCVB events are governed by the Southern New Hampshire Competitive Volleyball (SNHCVB) Rules 2024-2025. These rules cover indoor volleyball, co-ed play, RCO, and grass volleyball, and emphasize fairness, respect, and competitive integrity. The flexibility of these rules allows us to adapt to local needs and tournament formats, ensuring smooth operation at every event. Tournament-specific rules are provided in the event descriptions, with general guidelines applying unless otherwise specified.
